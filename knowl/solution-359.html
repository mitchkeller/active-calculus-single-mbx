Solution 6.1.5.1.1.

\(y=x^{1/2}\) is below the curve \(y=x^{1/4}\) for \(0\le x\le 1\text{,}\) as shown in the figure below (the bottom curve, in blue, is \(y = x^{1/2}\text{,}\) and the top, in black, is \(y = x^{1/4}\)).
(Click on the graph for a larger version.) Therefore,
\begin{equation*} \mbox{Area } = \int_{0}^{1} (x^{1/4} - x^{1/2}) dx = 0.133. \end{equation*}
The integral was evaluated on a calculator.
