Plotting \(f(x) = e^x\text{,}\) \(T_{10}(x)\text{,}\) and \(T_{20}(x)\) on the same axes, we observe that \(f(x)\) and \(T_{10}(x)\) are almost indistinguishable on the interval \(-4 \lt x \lt 4\text{,}\) and that when we consider \(T_{20}(x)\text{,}\) that interval widens to almost \(-8 \lt x \lt 8\text{.}\) For instance,
\begin{equation*} f(-4) - T_{10}(-4) \approx -0.0784 \end{equation*}
and
\begin{equation*} f(-8) - T_{20}(-8) \approx -0.1320\text{.} \end{equation*}
