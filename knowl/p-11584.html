Using the Taylor series for \(C(x)\text{,}\)
\begin{equation*} C(0.5) \approx \frac{1}{2} - \frac{1}{5 \cdot 2! \cdot 2^5} = \frac{159}{320} = 0.496875\text{,} \end{equation*}
which is accurate to within \(\frac{1}{9 \cdot 4! \cdot 2^9} = \frac{1}{110592} = 0.00000904 \ldots\text{.}\)
