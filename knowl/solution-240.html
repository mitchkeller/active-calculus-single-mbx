\(x^2y\text{,}\) and the constraints say that \(4x+y=108\text{,}\) so \(V(x) = x^2(108-4x)\text{.}\) We know \(x>0\text{,}\) also \(x\lt y=108-4x\) so \(5x\lt 108\text{,}\) hence \(x\lt 21.6\text{.}\) Thus the domain of \(V\) is \((0,21.6)\text{.}\) Note that \(V'(x)=216x-12x^2=12x(18-x)\text{,}\) so \(V'(x)>0\) and \(V\) is increasing when \(0\lt x\lt 18\text{,}\) and \(V'(x)\lt 0\) and \(V\) is decreasing when \(18\lt x\text{.}\) Thus the maximum volume occurs when \(x=18\) and \(y=36\text{,}\) giving a maximum of \(11664\) cubic inches.
