Solution 2.3.5.5.1.

A. By the product rule, \(G'(2) = F'(2)\cdot H(2) + F(2)\cdot H'(2) = 2\cdot 5 + 4\cdot 3 = 22\text{.}\) B. By the quotient rule,
\begin{equation*} G'(2) = \frac{F'(2)\cdot H(2) - F(2)\cdot H'(2)} {H(2)^2} = \frac{2\cdot 5 - 4\cdot 3}{5^2} \approx -0.08. \end{equation*}
