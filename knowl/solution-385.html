Solution 6.2.5.3.1.

\(\Delta x\text{,}\) outer radius \(y=1\) and inner radius \(y=x^{6}\text{.}\) Therefore,
\begin{equation*} \hbox{Volume of slice }\approx \pi 1^2\Delta x-\pi y^2\Delta x = \pi(1-x^{12})\, \Delta x. \end{equation*}
Therefore,
\begin{equation*} \hbox{Volume of solid } = \lim_{\Delta x\to0} \Sigma \pi(1-x^{12})\,\Delta x = \int_0^1\pi(1-x^{12})\, dx \end{equation*}
\begin{equation*} =\pi\left(x-\frac{x^{13}}{13}\right)\Bigg|_0^1 = \pi(1 - {1\over 13}) = {12\over13} \pi. \end{equation*}
