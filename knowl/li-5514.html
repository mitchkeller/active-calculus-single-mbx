Item 8.3.5.7.c.
For \(S_n = 1 + 2 + 4 + \cdots + 2^{n-1} \text{.}\)
  1. \(S_2 = \frac{1-2^2}{-1} = 3\text{,}\) \(S_3 = \frac{1-2^3}{-1} = 7\text{,}\) and \(S_4 = \frac{1-2^4}{-1} = 15\text{,}\) so \(S_n = 2^n - 1\text{.}\)
  2. The infinite geometric series \(1 + 2 + 4 + \cdots + 2^{n-1} + \cdots\) diverges.
