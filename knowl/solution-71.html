Solution 1.6.5.3.1.

\begin{equation*} v(t) = \lim_{h\to0} \frac{8\mathopen{}\left(t+h\right)^{2}+3 - 8t^{2}+3}{h} = \lim_{h\to0} \frac{8t^{2}+16th+8h^{2}+3-8t^{2}-3}{h} = \lim_{h\to0} 16t+8h = 16 t \end{equation*}
and
\begin{equation*} v(t) = \lim_{h\to0} \frac{8\cdot 2\mathopen{}\left(t+h\right) - 8\cdot 2t}{h} = \lim_{h\to0} \frac{16t+16h-16t}{h} = \lim_{h\to0} 16 = 16. \end{equation*}
