Solution 8.4.6.2.1.

\(\displaystyle \sum_{n=1}^\infty (-1)^{n-1} b_n\) satisfying the conditions of the alternating series test, i.e. 1. \(b_n \ge 0\) for all \(n\text{,}\) 2. \(b_{n+1} \le b_n\) for all \(n\text{,}\) 3. \(\displaystyle \lim_{n+1} b_n = 0\text{,}\) we have the error estimate \(|s_n - s| \le b_{n+1}\text{.}\) The given series satisfies the above conditions. Therefore we have to find the first term which is less than or equal 0.0000001. \(b_1 = 0.4 > 0.0000001\text{,}\) \(b_2 = \frac{(0.4)^3}{3!} = 0.0106666666666667 > 0.0000001\text{,}\) \(b_3 = \frac{(0.4)^5}{5!} = 8.53333333333334e-05 > 0.0000001\text{,}\) \(b_4 = \frac{(0.4)^7}{7!} = 3.25079365079365e-07 > 0.0000001\text{,}\) \(b_5 = \frac{(0.4)^9}{9!} = 7.22398589065256e-10 \lt 0.0000001\text{.}\) Therefore we have to compute the sum of the first 4 terms.
