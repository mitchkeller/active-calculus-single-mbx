Solution 8.3.5.1.1.

A. \(5 + 5 x + 5 x^2 + 5 x^4 + 5 x^8 + \cdots\) is not a geometric series. B. \(5 x^5 + 5 x^{6} + 5 x^{7} + 5 x^{8} + \cdots\) is a geometric series with first term \(5 x^{5}\) and ratio \(x\text{.}\) C. \(5 - 25 + 125 - 625 + 3125 - \cdots\) is a geometric series with first term \(5\) and ratio \(-5\text{.}\)
