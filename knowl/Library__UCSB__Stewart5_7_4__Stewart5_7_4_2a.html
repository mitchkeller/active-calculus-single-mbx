Exercise 1.

Which of the following is the correct form of the partial fraction decomposition of \(\displaystyle \frac{x-1}{x^3+x^2}?\) You may select more than one answer.
  1. \(\displaystyle \displaystyle \frac{Ax+B}{x}+\frac{Cx+D}{x^2}+\frac{E}{x+1}\)
  2. \(\displaystyle \displaystyle \frac{A}{x}+\frac{B}{x^2}+\frac{C}{x+1}\)
  3. \(\displaystyle \displaystyle \frac{Ax+B}{x}+\frac{Cx+D}{x^2}+\frac{Ex+F}{x+1}\)
  4. \(\displaystyle \displaystyle \frac{Ax+B}{x}+\frac{Cx+D}{x^2}+\frac{Ex+F}{x+1}\)
