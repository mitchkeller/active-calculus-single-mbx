Solution 2.7.3.1.1.

We differentiate implicitly with respect to \(x\text{.}\) This gives

\begin{equation*} 2 x y - x^{2} \, \frac{dy}{dx} - 1 - 5 \, \frac{dy}{dx} = 0, \end{equation*}

or

\begin{equation*} (x^{2}-5) \, \frac{dy}{dx} = 1-2xy, \end{equation*}

and so

\begin{equation*} \frac{dy}{dx} = \frac{1 - 2 x y}{x^{2} - 5}. \end{equation*}
