We have some flexibility in how we write an alternating series; for example, the series

\begin{equation*} \sum_{k=1}^{\infty} (-1)^{k+1} a_k\text{,} \end{equation*}

whose index starts at \(k = 1\text{,}\) is also alternating. As we will soon see, there are several very nice results that hold for alternating series, while alternating series can also demonstrate some unusual behavior.
