Solution 9.4.7.1.

\(x = \sin 7 t, \, y = \cos 7 t\text{,}\) hence \(x' = 7 \cos 7 t\) and \(y' = - 7 \sin 7 t\text{.}\) By the formula for the arc length we obtain:
\begin{equation*} S = \int ^\pi _0 \sqrt{x'(t)^2 + y'(t)^2} \, dt = \int ^\pi _0 \sqrt{49 \cos^2 7 t + 49 \sin^2 7 t} \, dt = \int ^\pi _0 \sqrt{49} \, dt = 7 \pi \end{equation*}
