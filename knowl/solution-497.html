Solution 8.2.4.4.1.

\begin{equation*} T(x) = f(a) + \frac{f'(a)}{1!} (x-a) + \frac{f''(a)}{2!} (x-a)^2 + ... + \frac{f^n(a)}{n!} (x-a)^n \end{equation*}
\begin{equation*} f'(x) = e^{x} \end{equation*}
\begin{equation*} f''(x) = e^{x} \end{equation*}
So, in this case,
\begin{equation*} T_2(x) = e^{0.1}+e^{0.1}\!\left(x-0.1\right)+\frac{e^{0.1}}{2}\!\left(x-0.1\right)^{2} \end{equation*}
\begin{equation*} |e^{x} - T_2(x)| = \left|e^{0.1}+e^{0.1}\cdot 0.4+\frac{e^{0.1}}{2}\cdot 0.16-e^{0.5}\right| = 0.0130683 \end{equation*}
