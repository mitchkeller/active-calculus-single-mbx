To find the mass of the first bar, we use the fact that \(M = \int_0^a \rho(x) \, dx\text{,}\) with \(a = 10\) and \(\rho(x) = \frac{1}{1+x^2}\text{.}\) Thus, the mass of the first bar is
\begin{equation*} M_1 = \int_0^{10} \frac{1}{1+x^2} \, dx = \left. \arctan(x) \right|_0^{10}\text{,} \end{equation*}
so \(M_1 = \arctan(10) \approx 1.47113\text{.}\)
