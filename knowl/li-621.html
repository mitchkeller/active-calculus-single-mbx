Item 1.5.4.7.a.

Observe that \(v(t) = 16-32t\) is a linear function with negative slope \(-32\text{,}\) and thus decreasing. Since the function is of interest from \(t = 0\) to \(t=2\text{,}\) the velocity is greatest at the left endpoint, when \(t=0\text{.}\) This greatest velocity is \(v(0) = 16\) feet per second.
