Solution 1.4.3.2.1.

\begin{equation*} g'(x) = \lim_{h \to 0} \frac{g(x+h)-g(x)}{h} = \lim_{h \to 0} \frac{4\!\left(x+h\right)^{2}-8-\left(4x^{2}-8\right)}{h} \end{equation*}
\begin{equation*} = \lim_{h \to 0} \frac{4(x^2+2xh+h^2)-8-4 x^2+8}{h} = \lim_{h \to 0} \frac{8 xh+2 h^2}{h} \end{equation*}
\begin{equation*} = \lim_{h \to 0}(8 x+2h) = 8 x. \end{equation*}
