Solution 1.8.4.6.1.

Solution: For small values of \(h\text{,}\) \(f'(c) \approx \frac{f(c+h) - f(c)}{h}\text{.}\) In this problem, \(c = -6.2\text{,}\) and \(h = -6 + 6.2 = 0.2\text{.}\)
\begin{equation*} f'(-6.2) \approx \frac{f(-6) - f(-6.2)}{0.2} = \frac{-6.4 + 2.7}{0.2} = -18.5. \end{equation*}
