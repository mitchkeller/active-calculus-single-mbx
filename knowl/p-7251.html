The derivative of \(x\) is \(1\) (which is of lower degree) while the derivative of \(\cos\mathopen{}\left(3x\right)\) is \(-3 \sin(3 x)\text{,}\) (which is another trig function and not any simpler). Thus, we should let \(u=x\) and \(dv=\cos\mathopen{}\left(3x\right)dx\text{.}\)
