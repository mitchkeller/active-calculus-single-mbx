Exercise 8.

A bungee jumper dives from a tower at time \(t=0\text{.}\) Her height \(s\) in feet at time \(t\) in seconds is given by \(s(t) = 100\cos(0.75t) \cdot e^{-0.2t}+100\text{.}\)
  1. Write an expression for the average velocity of the bungee jumper on the interval \([1,1+h]\text{.}\)
  2. Use computing technology to estimate the value of the limit as \(h \to 0\) of the quantity you found in (a).
  3. What is the meaning of the value of the limit in (b)? What are its units?
