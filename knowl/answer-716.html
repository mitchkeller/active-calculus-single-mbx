Answer 7.5.3.7.1.

  1. \(\displaystyle \frac{dv}{dt} = 9.8 - kv\)

  2. \(v = \frac{9.8}{k}\) is a stable equilibrium.

  3. \(\displaystyle v(t) = \frac{9.8 - 9.8e^{-kt}}{k}\)

  4. \(k = 9.8/54 \approx 0.181481\text{.}\)

  5. \(t = \frac{\ln(0.5)}{-0.181481} \approx 3.1894\) seconds.
