  2. Next, compute \(f(0)\text{,}\) \(f'(0)\text{,}\) and \(f''(0)\) and enter those values in Table 8.1.15.
  3. Use your work so far to determine the formula for \(T_1(x)\text{,}\) the tangent line approximation to \(f(x)\) at \(a =0\) (which satisfies \(T_1(0) = f(0)\) and \(T_1'(0) = f'(0)\)).
