Item a.

Since the mass, \(M\text{,}\) is given by \(M = \int_a^b \rho(x) \, dx\text{,}\) it follows that

\begin{equation*} M = \int_{0}^{10} 2e^{-0.2x} = \left. \frac{2}{-0.2}e^{-0.2x} \right|_{0}^{10} = -10e^{-2}+10e^{0}\text{,} \end{equation*}

and hence \(M = 10 - 10e^{-2} \approx 8.64665\) grams.
