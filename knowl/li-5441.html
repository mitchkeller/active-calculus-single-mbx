Item 8.3.7.6.c.
Since the terms in our series are all positive, we must have \(r \gt 0\text{.}\) Now the geometric series with ratio \(r\) converges only for \(r \lt 1\text{,}\)so the series
\begin{equation*} \sum a_k \end{equation*}
will converge if
\begin{equation*} \lim_{n \to \infty} \sqrt[n]{a_n} = r \end{equation*}
with \(0 \lt r \lt 1\text{.}\)
