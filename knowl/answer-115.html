Answer 1.6.2.1.

  1. Increasing: \(0\lt t\lt 2\text{,}\) \(3\lt t\lt 5\text{,}\) \(7\lt t\lt 9\text{,}\) and \(10\lt t\lt 12\text{.}\) Decreasing: never.
  2. Velocity is increasing on \(0\lt t\lt 1\text{,}\) \(3\lt t\lt 4\text{,}\) \(7\lt t\lt 8\text{,}\) and \(10\lt t\lt 11\text{;}\) \(y = v(t)\) is decreasing on \(1\lt t\lt 2\text{,}\) \(4\lt t\lt 5\text{,}\) \(8\lt t\lt 9\text{,}\) and \(11\lt t\lt 12\text{.}\) Velocity is constant on \(2\lt t\lt 3\text{,}\) \(5\lt t\lt 7\text{,}\) and \(9\lt t\lt 10\text{.}\)
  3. \(a(t) = v'(t)\) and \(a(t) = s''(t)\text{.}\)
  4. \(s''(t)\) is positive since \(s'(t)\) is increasing.
