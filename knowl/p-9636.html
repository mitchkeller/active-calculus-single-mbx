Consider the infinite Taylor series given by
\begin{align*} T(x) =\mathstrut \amp \sum_{k = 1}^{\infty} \frac{1}{k \cdot 2^k}(x-1)^k\\ =\mathstrut \amp \frac{1}{1 \cdot 2}(x-1) + \frac{1}{2 \cdot 4}(x-1)^2 + \frac{1}{3 \cdot 8}(x-1)^3 + \cdots + \frac{1}{n \cdot 2^n}(x-1)^n + \cdots\text{.} \end{align*}
