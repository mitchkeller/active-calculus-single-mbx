So the integral turns into
\begin{equation*} = 7 \int \bigg( u^2-1 \bigg) u^2 \sec(-15 x) \tan(-15 x) \frac{du}{-15\sec(-15 x)\tan(-15 x)} \end{equation*}
\begin{equation*} = \frac{7}{-15} \int \bigg( u^2-1 \bigg) u^2 \, du = \frac{7}{-15} \int \bigg( u^4-u^2 \bigg) \, du \end{equation*}
And each part can be integrated with power rule. Don't forget to plug back in what \(u\) equals!
