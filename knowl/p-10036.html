Integrating this last inequality, we obtain
\begin{equation*} \int_0^x -Mt \ dt \leq \int_0^x E^{(n)}_n(t) \ dt \leq \int_0^x Mt \ dt \end{equation*}
and thus
\begin{equation*} -M\frac{x^2}{2} \leq E^{(n-1)}_n(x) \leq M\frac{x^2}{2} \end{equation*}
