Item 7.4.3.8.c.
Since height decreases at \(20\) inches per minute when the water is filled to a depth of \(100\) inches, this tells us that \(\frac{dh}{dt}\vert_{h=100} = -20\text{.}\) Applying this information to the differential equation,
\begin{equation*} -20 = k \sqrt{100} \end{equation*}
and thus \(k = -5\text{.}\)
