Observe that
\begin{equation*} r_n(x) = \frac{\frac{1}{(n+1)!}x^{n+1}}{\frac{1}{n!}x^n} = \frac{n!}{(n+1)!} \cdot \frac{x^{n+1}}{x^n}\text{.} \end{equation*}
Since \(\frac{n!}{(n+1)!} = \frac{1}{n+1}\) and \(\frac{x^{n+1}}{x^n} = x\text{,}\) it follows that
\begin{equation*} r_n(x) = \frac{1}{n+1} \cdot x\text{.} \end{equation*}
