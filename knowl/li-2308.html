Item b.
Note that \(g''(x) \gt 0\) for \(x \lt -1\text{,}\) \(g''(x) \lt 0\) for \(-1 \lt x \lt 2\text{,}\) and \(g''(x) \lt 0\) for \(x \gt 2\text{.}\) This tells us that \(g\) is concave up for \(x \lt -1\text{,}\) concave down for \(-1 \lt x \lt 2\text{,}\) and concave down for \(x \gt 2\text{.}\)
