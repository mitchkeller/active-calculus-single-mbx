Solution 2.8.4.10.1.

\begin{equation*} \lim_{x\to a} \frac{f(x)}{g(x)} = \frac{f'(a)}{g'(a)}. \end{equation*}
For the left graph, since \(f'(a) \ne 0\) and \(g'(a) = 0\text{,}\) the limit is undefined.. For the right graph, since \(f'(a) = 0\) and \(g'(a) \ne 0\text{,}\) the limit is zero..
