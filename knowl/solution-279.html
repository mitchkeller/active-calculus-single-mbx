Solution 4.4.5.4.1.

Since the function \(f(x)=36 - x^2\) is even,
\begin{equation*} \int_{-6}^{6} (36 -x^2)\, dx = 2 \int_{0}^{6} (36 -x^2)\, dx = 2 \left. \left(36 x - \frac{x^3}{3}\right) \right|_ {0}^{6} = 2 \left[ \left(36 \cdot 6- \frac{6^3}{3}\right) - (0-0) \right] = 2 \frac{432}{3} = 288\text{.} \end{equation*}
