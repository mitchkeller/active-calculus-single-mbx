The formulas in parts (i), (ii), and (iii) represent the average rate of change of \(f(x)\) over the intervals \(0 \leq x \leq 1\text{,}\) \(1 \leq x \leq 3\text{,}\) and \(0 \leq x \leq 3\) respectively. Thus line segment D connecting the points \((0, 9)\) and \((1, 8)\) illustrates formula (i), line segment C connecting the points \((1, 8)\) and \((3, 0)\) illustrates formula (ii), and line segment B connecting the points \((0, 9)\) and \((3, 0)\) illustrates formula (iii).
