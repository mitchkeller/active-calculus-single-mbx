Solution 1.5.4.2.1.

(a) The statement \(f(100) = 250\) means that it costs 250 dollars to produce 100 gallons of ice cream. (b) The statement \(f'(100) = 1.2\) means that when the number of gallons produced is 100, costs are increasing by 1.2 dollars/gallon In other words, it costs about 1.2 dollars to produce the 101st gallon of ice cream.
