  3. \(\displaystyle h(y) = \frac{\cos(10y)}{e^{4y}+1}\)

  4. \(\displaystyle s(z) = 2^{z^2 \sec (z)}\)

  5. \(\displaystyle c(x) = \sin(e^{x^2})\)
