Solution 7.1.5.1.1.

\(\frac{d}{dx} (e^{-5 x}) = -5 e^{-5 x}\text{,}\) \(\frac{d^2}{dx^2} (e^{-5 x}) = 25 e^{-5 x}\text{,}\) \(\frac{d}{dx} (e^{5 x}) = 5 e^{-5 x}\text{,}\) \(\frac{d^2}{dx^2} (e^{-5 x}) = 25 e^{-5 x}\text{,}\) \(\frac{d}{dx} (\sin(5 x)) = 5 \cos(5 x)\text{,}\) \(\frac{d^2}{dx^2} (\sin(5 x)) = -25 \sin(5 x)\text{,}\) \(\frac{d}{dx} (5 \sin(x)) = 5 \cos(x)\text{,}\) and \(\frac{d^2}{dx^2} (5 \sin(x)) = -5 \sin(5 x)\text{.}\) Thus, the correct answers are: \(\frac{dy}{dx}=-5 y\) : E, \(\frac{dy}{dx}=5 y\) : F, \(\frac{d^2y}{dx^2}=25 y\) : A, and \(\frac{d^2y}{dx^2}=-25 y\) : C
