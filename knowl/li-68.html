Item 1.1.4.1.b.

The average velocity over the interval \(0.2 \le t \le 0.4\) is given by

\begin{equation*} \frac{s(0.4)-s(0.2)}{0.2}=\frac{1.4-0.5}{0.2}=4.5\text{ ft/s}. \end{equation*}

A reasonable estimate for the velocity at (t=0.2) is the average

\begin{equation*} \frac{1}{2}\cdot (2.5+4.5) = 3.5\text{ ft/s}\text{.} \end{equation*}
