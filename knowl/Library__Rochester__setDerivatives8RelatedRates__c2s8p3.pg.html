Exercise 8.

Water is leaking out of an inverted conical tank at a rate of \(12300.0\) \(\textrm{cm}^3/\textrm{min}\) at the same time that water is being pumped into the tank at a constant rate. The tank has height \(15.0 \ \textrm{m}\) and the the diameter at the top is \(5.0 \ \textrm{m}\text{.}\) If the water level is rising at a rate of \(20.0 \ \textrm{cm}/\textrm{min}\) when the height of the water is \(4.5 \ \textrm{m}\text{,}\) find the rate at which water is being pumped into the tank in cubic centimeters per minute.
Answer: \(\textrm{cm}^3/\textrm{min}\)
