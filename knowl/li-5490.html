Item.

A finite geometric series \(S_n\) is a sum of the form
\begin{equation*} S_n = a + ar + ar^2 + \cdots + ar^{n-1}\text{,} \end{equation*}
where \(a\) and \(r\) are real numbers such that \(r \ne 1\text{.}\) The value of the finite geometric series \(S_n\) can be computed directly as
\begin{equation*} S_n = \frac{a(1-r^n)}{1-r}\text{.} \end{equation*}
