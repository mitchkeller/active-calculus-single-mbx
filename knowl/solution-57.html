Solution 1.5.4.1.1.

(a) As the cup of coffee cools, the temperature decreases, so \(f'(t)\) is negative.
(b) Since \(f'(t) = dH/dt\text{,}\) the units are degrees Celsius per minute (degC/min). The quantity \(f'(5)\) represents the rate at which the coffee is cooling, in degrees per minute, 5 minutes after the cup is put on the counter. Thus we can complete the statement:
At 5 minutes after the coffee was put on the counter, its temperature is 80 degC and will decrease by about 2.1 degC in the next 90 seconds.
