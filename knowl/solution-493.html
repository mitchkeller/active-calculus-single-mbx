Solution 7.4.3.3.1.

\begin{equation*} \int\frac{dy}{y-250} = \int 0.4 dt, \end{equation*}
so
\begin{equation*} \ln|y-250| = 0.4 t +C. \end{equation*}
Solving for \(y\text{,}\)
\begin{equation*} y = 250 + A e^{0.4 t}, \end{equation*}
where \(A = \pm e^C\text{.}\) The initial condition, \(y(0)=60\text{,}\) gives \(60 = 250 + A\text{,}\) so \(A = 190\text{,}\) and
\begin{equation*} y= 250 - 190 e^{0.4 t}. \end{equation*}
