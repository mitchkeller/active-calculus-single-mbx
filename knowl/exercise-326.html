Exercise 5.

Match the solutions to the differential equations. If there is more than one solution to an equation, select the answer that includes all solutions.
  1. \(\displaystyle \frac{dy}{dx}=7 y\)
  2. \(\displaystyle \frac{d^2y}{dx^2}=-49 y\)
  3. \(\displaystyle \frac{dy}{dx}=-7 y\)
  4. \(\displaystyle \frac{d^2y}{dx^2}=49 y\)
  1. \(\displaystyle y = e^{-7 x}\)
  2. \(y = \sin(7 x)\) or \(y = 7\sin(x)\)
  3. \(\displaystyle y = e^{7 x}\)
  4. \(y = e^{-7 x}\) or \(y = e^{7 x}\)
  5. \(\displaystyle y = 7\sin(x)\)
  6. \(\displaystyle y = \sin(7 x)\)
