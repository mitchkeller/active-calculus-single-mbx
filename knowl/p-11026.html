Next, consider the finite geometric series that results when \(a = 1\) and \(r = -1\text{,}\) which is the sum
\begin{equation*} S_n = 1 - 1 + 1 - 1 + \cdots + (-1)^{n-1}\text{,} \end{equation*}
where there are \(n\) terms in the sum. (We view the first “\(1\)” as resulting from “\(1 \cdot (-1)^0\)”.)
