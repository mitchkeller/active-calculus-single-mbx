Answer 2.7.4.1.

  1. \(\frac{dy}{dx}(-3y^2 - 6x) = 6y-3x^2 \) and the tangent line has equation \(y - 3 = 1(x+3)\text{.}\)

  2. \(\frac{dy}{dx} = \frac{3x^2 + 1}{\cos(y) + 1}\) and the tangent line has equation \(y = \frac{1}{2}x\text{.}\)

  3. \(\frac{dy}{dx} = \frac{3e^{-xy} - 3xye^{-xy}}{3x^2e^{-xy}+2y}\) and the tangent line is \(y - 1 = 0.234950(x - 0.619061)\text{.}\)
