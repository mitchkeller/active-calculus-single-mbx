Solution 4.2.5.4.1.

Solution: Using the left endpoint Riemann sum, the total displacement is the net area of the light blue shaded rectangles, and the total distance is the absolute area of the light blue shaded rectangles. Total displacement = \(-2 - 3 - 1 - 2 - 4+1+2+4 = -5\) feet. Total distance = \(|-2|+|-3|+|-1|+|-2|+|-4|+|1|+|2|+|4| = 19\) feet. We can also make similar calculations using the right endpoint Riemann sum. Total displacement = \(-3 - 1 - 2 - 4+1+2+4+3 = 0\) feet. Total distance = \(|-3|+|-1|+|-2|+|-4|+|1|+|2|+|4|+|3| = 20\) feet.
