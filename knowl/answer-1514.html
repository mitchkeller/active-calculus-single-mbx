Answer 8.3.2.1.

  1. \(\frac{2}{5} \cdot S_n = \frac{2}{5} + \left(\frac{2}{5}\right)^2 \left(\frac{2}{5}\right)^3 + \cdots + \left(\frac{2}{5}\right)^n \text{.}\)
  2. The key observation is that \(1 + \frac{2}{5} + \left(\frac{2}{5}\right)^2 + \cdots + \left(\frac{2}{5}\right)^{n-1} - \frac{2}{5} - \left(\frac{2}{5}\right)^2 - \left(\frac{2}{5}\right)^3 - \cdots - \left(\frac{2}{5}\right)^n = 1 - \left(\frac{2}{5}\right)^n\text{,}\)
  3. \(\displaystyle S_n = \frac{1 - \left(\frac{2}{5}\right)^n}{\frac{3}{5}}\)
  4. \(\displaystyle S_n = \frac{7 - 7 \cdot \left(\frac{2}{5}\right)^n}{\frac{3}{5}}\)
