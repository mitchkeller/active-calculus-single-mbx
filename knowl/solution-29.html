Solution 1.3.3.2.1.

\(B=(6.8,2)\) is on the graph of \(g\text{,}\) we have \(g(6.8)=2\text{.}\) The slope of the tangent line touching the graph at \(x=2\) is given by
\begin{equation*} \mbox{slope} = \frac{\mbox{rise}}{\mbox{run}} = \frac{2-2.05}{6.8-6.74} = -0.833. \end{equation*}
Thus, \(g'(6.8)=-0.833\text{.}\)
