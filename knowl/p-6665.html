Note that \(A\) is increasing wherever \(f\) is positive, and \(A\) is CCU wherever \(f\) is increasing. Similar conclusions follow for \(A\) being decreasing and/or concave down. Moreover, \(A(2) = 0\text{,}\) \(A(3) = -0.5\text{,}\) \(A(4) = -1.5\text{,}\) \(A(5) = -2\text{,}\) \(A(6) = -2 + \frac{\pi}{4}\text{,}\) and \(A(7) = -2 + \frac{\pi}{2}\text{.}\)
