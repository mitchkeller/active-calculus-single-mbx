Solution 6.5.5.2.1.

\(w = 1 + e^{5 x}\text{,}\) we have
\begin{equation*} \int_{-\infty}^{-1}\,{e^{5 x}\over 1 + e^{5 x}}\,dx = \lim\limits_{b\to 1^{+}} \int_b^{1 + e^{-5}} {1\over 5}{dw\over w} = \lim\limits_{b\to 1^{+}} {1\over 5}(\ln(1 + e^{-5}) - \ln(b)) = {1\over 5}\ln(1 + e^{-5}). \end{equation*}
