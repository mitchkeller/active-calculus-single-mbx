Solution 1.5.4.2.1.

(a) The statement \(f(125) = 325\) means that it costs 325 dollars to produce 125 gallons of ice cream. (b) The statement \(f'(125) = 1.2\) means that when the number of gallons produced is 125, costs are increasing by 1.2 dollars/gallon In other words, it costs about 1.2 dollars to produce the 126th gallon of ice cream.
