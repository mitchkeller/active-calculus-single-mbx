\(f'(3)\) using the average rate of change formula on a small interval around 3. Let's try the interval from \(x = 3\) to \(x = 3 + 0.0001\text{.}\) Then we have
\begin{equation*} f'(3) \approx {f(3+0.0001) - f(3)\over 0.0001} = {6^{3.0001} - 6^{3}\over 0.0001} \approx 387, \end{equation*}

\begin{equation*} f'(3) \approx {f(3+0.00001) - f(3)\over 0.00001} = {6^{3.00001} - 6^{3}\over 0.00001} \approx 387. \end{equation*}
This agrees with our previous result to one decimal place, so \(f'(3)\approx 387\) is a good estimate.
