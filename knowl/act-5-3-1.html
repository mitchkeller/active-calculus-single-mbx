Activity 5.3.2.

  1. \(\displaystyle \int \sin(8-3x) \, dx\)
  2. \(\displaystyle \int \sec^2 (4x) \, dx\)
  3. \(\displaystyle \int \frac{1}{11x - 9} \, dx\)
  4. \(\displaystyle \int \csc(2x+1) \cot(2x+1) \, dx\)
  5. \(\displaystyle \int \frac{1}{\sqrt{1-16x^2}}\, dx\)
  6. \(\displaystyle \int 5^{-x}\, dx\)
