\begin{align*} f'(x) &= \lim_{h \to 0} \frac{f(x+h)-f(x)}{h}\\ &= \lim_{h \to 0} \frac{\left[(x+h)^2-3\right] - \left[x^2-3\right]}{h}\\ &= \lim_{h \to 0} \frac{\left[x^2+2xh+h^2-3\right] - \left[x^2-3\right]}{h}\\ &= \lim_{h \to 0} \frac{2xh+h^2}{h}\\ &= \lim_{h \to 0} 2x+h\\ &= 2x \end{align*}
