Exercise 1. Limits of five sequences.

Match the formulas with the descriptions of the behavior of the sequence as \(n\to\infty\text{.}\)
  1. \(\displaystyle s_n = n\sin(n)/(n+1)\)
  2. \(\displaystyle s_n = 1 + \cos(n)/n\)
  3. \(\displaystyle s_n = (n+1)/n\)
  4. \(\displaystyle s_n = 1 - n^2\)
  5. \(\displaystyle s_n = 3 - 1/n\)
  1. does not converge, but doesn't go to \(\pm\infty\)
  2. converges to one from above and below
  3. converges to one from above
  4. converges to three from below
  5. diverges to \(-\infty\)
