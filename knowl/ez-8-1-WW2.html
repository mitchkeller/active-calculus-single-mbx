Exercise 1. Limits of five sequences.

Match the formulas with the descriptions of the behavior of the sequence as \(n\to\infty\text{.}\)

  1. \(\displaystyle s_n = (n+1)/n\)

  2. \(\displaystyle s_n = n(n+1) - 1\)

  3. \(\displaystyle s_n = 3 + (-1)^n/(n)\)

  4. \(\displaystyle s_n = \cos(1/n)\)

  5. \(\displaystyle s_n = (\sin(n)/n)\)

  1. diverges to \(\infty\)

  2. converges to zero through positive and negative numbers

  3. converges to one from above

  4. converges to three from above and below

  5. converges to one from below
