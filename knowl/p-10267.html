The integral involves \(\cot u\) and \(\csc u\) where the exponent of \(\cot u\) is odd and positive. So we rewrite in order to have a copy of \(\csc(4 x) \cot(4 x)\) and use a trigonometric identity to turn the remaining even powers of \(\cot(4 x)\) into an expression of \(\csc^2(4 x)\text{.}\) Then use u-substitution with \(u= \csc(4 x)\text{.}\)
