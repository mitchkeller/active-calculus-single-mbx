Solution 4.4.5.2.1.

\(\displaystyle \int u^n du = \frac{u^{n+1}}{n+1}\text{:}\)
\begin{equation*} \parbox{0.4\textwidth}{ \ \begin{aligned} \int_{2}^{4} \frac 1 {x^2} dx \amp = \int_{2}^{4} x^{-2} dx \\\\ \amp = \left (-1)x^{-1} \right|_{2}^{4} = \left \frac{-1}{x} \right|_{2}^{4} \\\\ \amp = \frac{-1}{4} - \frac{-1}{2} = \frac 1 {2} - \frac 1 {4} = 0.25 \end{aligned} } \end{equation*}
