Let \(p(z)\) be given by the rule
\begin{equation*} p(z) = \frac{z\tan(z)}{z^2\sec(z) + 1} + 3 e^z + 1\text{.} \end{equation*}
  1. Determine \(p'(z)\text{.}\)
  2. Find an equation for the tangent line to \(p\) at the point where \(z = 0\text{.}\)
  3. At \(z = 0\text{,}\) is \(p\) increasing, decreasing, or neither? Why?
