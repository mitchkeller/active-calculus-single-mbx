Item b.
For \(h(x) = \ln(1+x)\text{:}\)
  1. \(p(u) = \frac{1}{1-u} = 1 + u + u^2 + u^3 + \cdots\) and this series converges for \(|u| \lt 1\text{.}\)
  2. \(r(x) = \frac{1}{1+x} = p(-x) = 1 - x + x^2 - x^3 + \cdots\text{.}\)
  3. \(\ln(1 + x) = x - \frac{1}{2}x^2 + \frac{1}{3} x^3 - \frac{1}{4}x^4 + \cdots \text{;}\) and this series is guaranteed to converge on \(-1 \lt x \lt 1\text{.}\)
