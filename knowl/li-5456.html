Item a.
We see that
\begin{equation*} \frac{2}{5} \cdot S_n = \frac{2}{5} \left( 1 + \frac{2}{5} + \left(\frac{2}{5}\right)^2 + \cdots + \left(\frac{2}{5}\right)^{n-1} \right) \text{,} \end{equation*}
or
\begin{equation} \frac{2}{5} \cdot S_n = \frac{2}{5} + \left(\frac{2}{5}\right)^2 \left(\frac{2}{5}\right)^3 + \cdots + \left(\frac{2}{5}\right)^n \text{.}\tag{8.3.5} \end{equation}
