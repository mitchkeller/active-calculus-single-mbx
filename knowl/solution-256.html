\begin{equation*} \int_{0}^{1}\,e^{x}\,dx \approx e^{0}\cdot 0.5 + e^{0.5}\cdot 0.5 \end{equation*}

\begin{equation*} \int_{0}^{1}\,e^{x}\,dx \approx e^{0.5}\cdot 0.5 + e^{1}\cdot 0.5. \end{equation*}
From the sketches we can see that the right Riemann sum is an overestimate and the left Riemann sum an underestimate.
