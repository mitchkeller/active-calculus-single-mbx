\begin{equation*} \frac{2}{25} x + \frac{2}{4} y \frac{dy}{dx} = 0, \end{equation*}
so the slope is \({dy\over dx} = -\frac{4 x}{25 y}\text{.}\) The slope is not defined anywhere along the line \(y=0\text{.}\) This ellipse intersects that line in two places, \((-5,0)\) and \((5,0)\text{.}\) (These are the ``ends’’ of the ellipse where the tangent is vertical.)
