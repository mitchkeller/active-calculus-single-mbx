For instance, in Preview Activity 8.1.1, we found that \(|f(x)-T_1(x)| \lt 0.1\) for approximately \(-0.5 \lt x \lt 0.4\text{.}\) The quadratica approximation is much better: we note that \(|f(x)-T_2(x)| \lt 0.1\) for approximately \(-0.9 \lt x \lt 0.8\text{,}\) and for any \(x\)-value in that interval, \(|f(x)-T_2(x)| \lt |f(x)-T_1(x)|\text{.}\)
