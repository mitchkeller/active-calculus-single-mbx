Solution 7.4.3.3.1.

\begin{equation*} \int\frac{dy}{y-250} = \int 0.6 dt, \end{equation*}
so
\begin{equation*} \ln|y-250| = 0.6 t +C. \end{equation*}
Solving for \(y\text{,}\)
\begin{equation*} y = 250 + A e^{0.6 t}, \end{equation*}
where \(A = \pm e^C\text{.}\) The initial condition, \(y(0)=70\text{,}\) gives \(70 = 250 + A\text{,}\) so \(A = 180\text{,}\) and
\begin{equation*} y= 250 - 180 e^{0.6 t}. \end{equation*}
