Solution 8.3.5.2.1.

A. The series \(5 + 5 (0.25) + 5 (0.25)^2 + \cdots + 5 (0.25)^{15}\) has 16 terms, and its sum is
\begin{equation*} 5 + 5 (0.25) + 5 (0.25)^2 + \cdots + 5 (0.25)^{15} = {5 (1 - 0.25^{16})\over 1 - 0.25}. \end{equation*}
B. The series \(5 (0.25)^{6} + 5 (0.25)^{7} + 5 (0.25)^{8} + \cdots + 5 (0.25)^{12}\) has \(12 - 6 + 1 = 7\) terms. We can write it as
\begin{equation*} 5 (0.25)^{6} + 5 (0.25)^{7} + 5 (0.25)^{8} + \cdots + 5 (0.25)^{12} = (5 (0.25)^{6}) (1 + 0.25 + \cdots + 0.25^{6}), \end{equation*}
so its sum is
\begin{equation*} (5 (0.25)^{6}) (1 + 0.25 + \cdots + 0.25^{6}) = {5 (0.25)^{6} (1 - 0.25^{7})\over 1 - 0.25}. \end{equation*}
