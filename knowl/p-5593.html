To estimate the total distance traveled by the object on \([1,5]\text{,}\) we have to calculate the total area between the curve and the \(t\)-axis. Thus,
\begin{equation*} D \approx \frac{7}{25} \cdot \frac{4}{5} + \frac{17}{25} \cdot \frac{4}{5} + 1 \cdot \frac{4}{5} + \frac{17}{25} \cdot \frac{4}{5} + \frac{7}{25} \cdot \frac{4}{5} = \frac{292}{125} \approx 2.336\text{.} \end{equation*}
