The denominator factors as \(x^3 + x^2 = x^2 (x+1)\text{.}\) Since the denominator factors in the linear term \(x\) repeated two times, and in the linear term \(x+1\text{,}\) the correct form of the partial fraction is:
\begin{equation*} \displaystyle \frac{A}{x}+\frac{B}{x^2}+\frac{C}{x+1} \end{equation*}
Thus the correct answer is \(\displaystyle \frac{A}{x}+\frac{B}{x^2}+\frac{C}{x+1}\text{.}\)
