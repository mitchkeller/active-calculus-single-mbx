Solution 1.1.4.1.1.

\(0 \le t \le 0.2\) is given by \(\frac{s(0.2)-s(0)}{0.2}=\frac{0.5}{0.2}=2.5\) ft/s. The average velocity over the interval \(0.2 \le t \le 0.4\) is given by \(\frac{s(0.4)-s(0.2)}{0.2}=\frac{1.4-0.5}{0.2}=4.5\) ft/s. A reasonable estimate for the velocity at \(t=0.2\) is the average \(\frac{1}{2}\cdot (2.5+4.5) = 3.5\) ft/s.
