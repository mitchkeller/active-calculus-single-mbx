Solution 7.4.3.3.1.

\begin{equation*} \int\frac{dy}{y-150} = \int 0.7 dt, \end{equation*}
so
\begin{equation*} \ln|y-150| = 0.7 t +C. \end{equation*}
Solving for \(y\text{,}\)
\begin{equation*} y = 150 + A e^{0.7 t}, \end{equation*}
where \(A = \pm e^C\text{.}\) The initial condition, \(y(0)=35\text{,}\) gives \(35 = 150 + A\text{,}\) so \(A = 115\text{,}\) and
\begin{equation*} y= 150 - 115 e^{0.7 t}. \end{equation*}
